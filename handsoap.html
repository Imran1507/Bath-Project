About Body Care from Bath & Body Works

Lotions, scrubs, creams, mists, oils…we get it, body care can be overwhelming. That’s why we created a step-by-step
skincare guide (and some fabulous products) to get you started:

It all starts with exfoliation. Our body scrubs buff away dead skins (and bad moods), leaving you soft, smooth and primed
for the rest of your body care routine.

Next, get into the cleansing mood with a dreamily scented body wash or shower gel. From floral to fresh, citrusy to warm,
you’ll find the perfect wake-up call…even if the alarm did go off a little too early.

Now, add some moisture to your skin with either body cream or body lotion. What’s the difference? Our body lotions are
packed with super-fluffy shea butter and hydrating vitamin E to keep you incredibly soft, smooth and nourished. Our
body cream is also super-hydrating, but with a lush mix of glow-giving coconut oil, moisturizing shea butter and
super-rich cocoa butter. Bonus: they leave you feeling smooth and refreshed, without feeling oily or heavy.

And the best for last: it’s time to indulge! Add a lip gloss or balm to your routine to keep your lips soft. Finish off the
process with a spritz of your favorite fine fragrance mist to complement your mood.

What if you want to spend a Saturday afternoon relaxing in the tub? Perfect! Light your favorite Bath & Body Works 3-wick
candle, grab a matching (or completely different!) bubble bath or bath fizzie (think: bath bomb) and soak up some suds
and good vibes.

Explore more body care and skincare routine products made for specifics: like Aromatherapy, foot care or hand care.
Don’t forget that your hair and face need TLC, too!
